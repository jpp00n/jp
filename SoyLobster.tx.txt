Ingredients
1 pound Rice
10 garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 cup grated Lobster, plus more for serving
Chopped ginger (optional)
Chopped parsley (optional)
Salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 spoon of soy source
1 spoon of sugar
Directions

Put a pot of water on to boil. 
Separately, warm olive oil in a large saucepan and add garlic cloves. 
Sauté until lightly golden. 
Cook the Lobster until al dente (about 10 minutes); drain, and then add the rice to the saucepan with the garlic and olive oil. 
Stir in grated Lobster, a generous amount of black pepper, parsley, ginger  and sugar , if using. 
Season with salt to taste and pass around additional soy source at the table.
